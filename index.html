Berry Clafoutis

Click thumbnails for step-by-step instructions:

By: Julia Child

Fresh raspberries baked in a cream clafoutis topped with sprinkled sugar. Surprisingly easy to make and incredibly delicious!

40-45 minutes

Bowl mixer preferred, but whisk is fine!

If you cannot find caster sugar, run granulated sugar in a food processor for a few minutes as a substitute
